# Prohibited Use

Do not use this repository for:

- Diagnosis
- Treatment recommendation
- Treatment planning
- Prognosis
- Second opinions
- Patient-facing medical advice
- Medical device use
- Clinical deployment
- Emergency care or triage
- Replacing licensed clinical judgment

Do not add, submit, upload, link, or reference:

- PHI
- PII
- Patient data
- Clinic identifiers
- Internal discovery logs
- Private protocol drafts
- Raw research archives
- Any material that could identify a patient, clinician, clinic, hospital, or care site

This repository is for portfolio and research demonstration only.
