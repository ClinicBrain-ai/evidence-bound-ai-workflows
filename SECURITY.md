# Security And Privacy

## Reporting Safety Or Privacy Concerns

If you identify a safety, privacy, or content-boundary concern in this repository, report it through the repository owner's preferred contact channel after the repository is published.

Do not include PHI, PII, patient data, clinic identifiers, screenshots of clinical records, or private clinical details in issues, pull requests, messages, or attachments.

## Content Boundary

This repository should contain only synthetic or de-identified research and portfolio material.

The repository should not contain:

- PHI
- PII
- Patient data
- Clinic identifiers
- Internal discovery logs
- Private protocol drafts
- Raw research archives

## Clinical Safety Boundary

This repository is not for diagnosis, treatment recommendation, prognosis, second opinions, medical device use, clinical deployment, or patient-facing medical advice.
